University

University talks are on Wednesday and are in-depth 3 hour sessions with a 30 minute refreshment break.

University Day attendees can also attend Hackergarten, BOFs and Hands-on Lab sessions on this day too.

Hackergarten

Hackergartens are a great place to meet new people, have fun, and improve the lives of developers around the world one patch at a time; provided you're willing to bring your laptop and get your hands dirty. Write a plugin, a tutorial, a small feature, or a bug fix for a project that interests you alongside several open-source experts willing to share their knowledge.

Labs

Practical hands-on sessions of 3 hours with maximum of 35 people per room. You will need to bring your own laptop and be prepared to preload software so that you can get moving right away.

Conference

Conference talks are 50 minutes in duration and take place on Thursday and Friday.

Conference Day attendees can also attend Quickies, BOFs and Hackergarten on Thursday and Friday.

Quickies

15 minute sessions during the lunch breaks.

BOFs

Informal evening sessions of 1 hour designed for audience participation and discussions.
